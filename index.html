    About Us

    Who we Are

    We are a social enterprise registered in Kenya, that aims at greating partnerships with communities as a microfinance whose mandate is to alleviate poverty, improving economic growth and livelihood of its people with financial inclusion among youth, men and women who are running Micro, Small and Medium enterprises in kenya

    Our Mission

    To support enterprenure owned Micro,Small and Medium Enterprises through provision of micro credit and training.

    Our Vision

    To empower youth and women to secure dignified social economic opportunies through creating employement for themselve and the societies they live in.

    Why Choose Us

    At P & G SWIFT Management LTD, we provide accessible, affordable, and flexible financial solutions tailored to your needs. Our mission is to empower individuals and businesses with the resources they need to succeed, backed by transparent and reliable service.

    • Empowerment – Helping you achieve financial independence and growth.
    • Integrity – Operating with honesty and transparency in all our services.
    • Sustainability – Supporting long-term success through responsible financial practices.
